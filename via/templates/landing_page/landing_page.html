More About Simplify.ai

This platform is very helpful for businesses that perform image and video annotation task.

Using this platform, a manager can create batches of images and videos and assign these batches to his/her team. We also provide intuitive analytics so that the manager knows what is the status of the assigned batches.

The operator is required to do the annotation task for the image or video.

How It Works

STEP 1

Register your company with us.

STEP 2

Add managers and operators in your company.

STEP 3

Manager will create batches of images and videos and assign them to respective operators.

STEP 4

Operator will complete the annotation task and report the completion to his manager.
